Road accidents and its safety measures

Introduction

Road accident, also called motor vehicle collision, car accident, or car crash, occurs when a vehicle collides with another vehicle, pedestrian, animal, road debris, or other stationary obstruction, such as a tree, pole or building. Traffic collisions often result in injury, disability, death, and property damage as well as financial costs to both society and the individuals involved.

2. Multiple vehicle collision

3. Vehicle rollover

9. Distracted driving

10. Hit and Run

11. Chain Reaction Driving

12. Failure to yield

13. Accident at low speed
